MCA Entrance Combo Course
Introduction:

This is a featured combo pack which has been designed specially for students in their second year/ 3rd semester of graduation who want to have an early start so that their preparation for MCA Entrance is done at a comfortable pace without affecting their college studies.

In addition to all the regular features of an association with SANMACS they get the added advantage of being taught their University Mathematics syllabus. Major portion of their College Mathematics syllabus will be covered by the experts of Academy of Mathematical sciences with the dual objective of an excellent preparation for their college Mathematics papers and the strong foundation for their MCA entrance exam. In addition regular classes will also be conducted for MCA entrance.

This course is also useful for students who have completed their graduation, as most of the topics of University Mathematics syllabus are important for MCA Entrance Examinations.

The course is spread over 2 years and the classes are conducted @ 6 hrs/week.

In addition to the above, an extensive online support is also provided by posting extra notes, tests, material, information and detailed test analysis on a specially designed log in accessible area on www.sanmacs.com.

Meant For:

Second year students / Third semester students / Graduates willing to prepare thoroughly
