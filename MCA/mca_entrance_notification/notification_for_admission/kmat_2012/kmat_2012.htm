MCA ENTRANCE NOTIFICATION

 KARNATAKA MANAGEMENT APTITUDE TEST (KMAT 2012)

Admission for M.C.A. 2012

KMAT MCA Entrance Test Date: Sunday, 22nd July 2012       Timings: 2pm to 5pm

How to Apply :
You can download application form from website.

a. Candidates who are applying for either MBA or MCA have to prepare a DD of Rs.500/-.

b. Candidates who are applying for both MCA & MBA have to prepare a DD of Rs.600/-.

Please make a photocopy of the application form and DD as a proof of acknowledgement.
DO NOT STAPLE THE PHOTOGRAPH
DO NOT GET THE PHOTOGRAPH ATTESTED
