MCA ENTRANCE NOTIFICATION

 Tezpur University, Assam - 2013

Admission for M.C.A. 2013

Testdate: June 1, 2013        Test Time : 10 AM to 12 Noon

Eligibility:
Bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50% marks in major / honours subject or 55% marks in aggregate for those candidates having no major/honours. Passed in Mathematics at 10+2 level.

Test Centers: Agartala, Bhubaneswar, Bangalore, Chennai, Delhi, Dibrugarh, Diphu, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Imphal, Itanagar, Jorhat, Kokrajhar, Kolkata, Lucknow, Mumbai, North Lakhimpur, Pune, Patna, Siliguri, Shillong, Silchar, and Tezpur.

Intake: 60

Exam Pattern:
The written test consists of three parts:
(i) Logical Reasoning and Basic Mathematical Ability,
(ii) Mathematics (10+2 level) or fundamentals of Computer Science and
(iii) English composition.

How to Apply:
TU application form along with the prospectus can be obtained
(i) from the designated branches of PUNJAB NATIONAL BANK by paying Rs.510/- (Rs.260/- for SC/ST)
or
(ii) by sending a request to the Controller of Examination, T.U. with a demand draft of Rs.560/- (Rs.310/- for SC/ST) in favour of the Registrar, Tezpur University, payable at Tezpur, with a self addressed unstamped envelope of 36 cm x 25 cm.,
or
(iii) downloading the application forms from the website (www.tezu.ernet.in). Downloaded application forms should be accompanied by a draft drawn in favour of the Registrar, Tezpur University payable at Tezpur for Rs 500/ ( Rs 250/- for SC/ST).

Candidates applying for more than one programme should apply separately for each programme. However, candidates applying for Integrated M.Sc./ Integrated B.Sc.B.Ed programme under a particular Department need not apply in separate application form. Similarly, for Integrated MA/ Integrated B.A.B.Ed. (English major in BA) programme candidate needs to apply in a single application form.
