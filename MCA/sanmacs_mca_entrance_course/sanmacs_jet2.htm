 INTRODUCTION

The duration of the course is approximately 80 - 100 hours spread over 1.5 - 2 months. The classes are discussion and problem solving oriented and are usually conducted 3 days a week of 4 hrs. duration each(with sufficient break). This crash course is specially designed for those students who have done the preparation themselves but are willing to correct their shortcomings and increase their chances of selection manifolds with SANMACS touch.
This is a course which is immaculately designed with all SANMACS expertise to help a reasonably prepared student to fulfill his dream of getting into a good MCA with SANMACS help. This course is also useful for students/working professionals who are not having enough time to join the comprehensive course but are willing to sharpen their competitive edges.
