Eligibility to Appear in UPSEE-2013:
For admission to first year of MCA in any institution affiliated to GBTU, Lucknow & MTU, Noida a candidate must have passed Bachelors degree course of three years minimum duration from any recognized Indian University; or its equivalent, recognized by A.I.U. securing minimum 50% (45% for SC/ST) marks in aggregate. Candidate must have passed Mathematics at 10+2 level or Graduation level.

Reservation of Seats in UPSEE- 2013:
85% seats are available in MCA & MAM (dual degree) course at all Private Institutions will be filled by the UPSEE-2013 qualified candidates, who have passed the qualifying examination from any institution located in U.P. or whose parents are domicile of U.P subject to the eligibility conditions given in this information brochure. If the candidate has passed qualifying examination from outside of U.P., he/she has to produce domicile certificate of his/her parents (Father OR Mother only) before the starting date of counselling.

Purchase and Submission of Application Forms:

  1. APPLICATION PROCEDURE: The online application can be filled up from 10.A.M.,28th Feb., 2013 till 10.00 P.M. of 21st March,2013 through the website http://upsee.nic.in or http://uptu.ac.in as per detailed instructions given on the websites. For assistance (in case of any difficulty) in filling up ON-LINE application form, candidate may contact the felicitation centres at various cities. The list of felicitation centres shall be made available on website.

    1. The applicants should submit all the details as per their eligibility while filling the online application form. After filling online application form; you are required to review all the entries filled by you by clicking on “review button” before submitting it. On submission of application, a registration number will be generated. You are required to note this number for all future correspondence.

    2. Now you may pay the required examination fee through either bank challan or credit/ debit card / internet banking.

      1. If you are opting to pay examination fee through bank challan, click the required link (download e-challan) to generate the challan in triplicate. The challan will contain your credentials along with the required fees. You should take the challan along with your signature and the requisite fees to the designated bank for the payment on next day (Day 2). If you have submitted application form after 17.00 hrs then the e-challan payments will be accepted by the bank on Day 3.
        The bank will provide you a transaction number, make sure that the transaction number is filled up correctly at the space provided on the challan.

      2. To generate the confirmation page (if the payment made through challan) visit the website http://upsee.nic.in or http://uptu.ac.in again further on next day (Day 3 or Day 4)) and click the link “Generate the Confirmation Page (e-challan)”. Fill up registration number and transaction number (provided by bank) to generate the confirmation page.

      3. If you are making payment through credit card / debit card / internet banking then click an appropriate link and make the payment. All VISA/Master cards are accepted. You should be ready with your credit, debit card / internet banking details. Make the payment and generate the confirmation page.

    3. Take the printout of the confirmation page, paste your recent passport size photograph, put your signature & thumb impression (left thumb), parent/ guardian signature at the space provided on the confirmation page. Post the duly signed completed confirmation page along with copy of challan (if the payment is made through bank challan) so as to reach the Registrar (UPSEE-2013), Gautam Buddh Technical University, IET Campus, Sitapur Road, Lucknow – 226021, Uttar Pradesh on or before 25th March, 2013 by 5.00 pm. Please do not forget to mention your mailing address, contact phone number at the space provided on the confirmation page.

  2. The Cost of Information Brochure and application form including examination fee for ON-LINE APPLICATION SUBMISSION will be as under:-

    General/ OBC Category (Orange colour)

    SC/ST Category and All Female Candidate (Magenta colour)

    Rs. 1000/-

    Rs. 500/-

  3. Completed confirmation page along with Bank Challan (if payment is made through Bank Challan) must reach to Registrar, (UPSEE-2013), Gautam Buddh Technical University, I.E.T. Campus, Sitapur Road, Lucknow-226021 upto March 25, 2013, 5.00 p.m. by Speed Post / Registered Post only. The application form delivered by any other means shall not be accepted. The application forms received in the University after the last date will not be accepted.
