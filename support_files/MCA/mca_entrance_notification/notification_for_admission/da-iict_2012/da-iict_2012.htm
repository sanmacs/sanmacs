MSC (IT) ENTRANCE NOTIFICATION

 Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information & Communication Technology

MSC (IT) Admission - 2012

Testdate: 3 June 2012

Eligibility:
A graduate with a minimum of 60% marks in their qualifying degree examination may apply. Candidates appearing in their final degree examination and expecting to complete it by July 2012 may also apply subject to the condition that they obtain the 60% cumulative record after final examination and submit the provisional degree certificate or any other proof of the completion of degree certificate before 30 October 2012 to the Institute. The students must have at least three courses of Mathematics, Statistics or Computer science in their undergraduate curriculum.
The decision of the concerned authorities of DA-IICT regarding eligibility of any candidate shall be final

Number of Seats: Maximum number of seats available is 90.

Selection Process:
Candidates for admission will be selected through an Entrance Test of the Institute. The test will be of one hour duration. The test will evaluate the candidates on their mathematical aptitude, logical reasoning, basic programming, English and general awareness of Information technology. Final merit list for admission will be prepared based on the performance of the Entrance Test only.
The admit card for the Entrance Test will be sent to the candidates by post by 25 May 2012. The result of the Entrance Test will be announced at this website by Second week of June 2012. For further updates on selection process, keep visiting our website http://www.daiict.ac.in

Test Centers:
Ahmedabad, Bhopal, Chennai, DAIICT Gandhinagar, Hyderabad, Jaipur, Kolkata, Lucknow, Mumbai, Nagpur, New Delhi, Pune, Rajkot, Raipur, Surat, Udaipur, Vadodara

Application Procedure
Candidates will be required to submit online (preferably) application form by clicking on the link given below. Admission fee (non-refundable) is Rs. 1000/- for online and Rs.1100/- for offline. The candidates who submit an online admission form are required to send a demand draft of Rs. 1000/- drawn in favour “DA-IICT” and payable at Ahmedabad, to the address given below. Candidates downloading the form and sending it by post have to send a demand draft of Rs. 1100/- in favour of “DA-IICT” and payable at Ahmedabad and attach it with the application form.

Candidate must write his/her name, address, contact number (s), Program name, and application number (if application form has been submitted online) on the reverse side of the bank draft.

Candidates need to indicate their research interests in the online application form. Candidates may provide more than one interests. Candidates are advised to look at profile of several research groups available at this website, for identifying the group that has closest match with their own research interests.
