WEST BENGAL JOINT ENTRANCE EXAMINATIONS - JECA 2009

MCA Admission 2009-10

Testdate: 28th June, 2009             Timings: 12 noon to 2-30 p.m.

Eligibility:
candidates who are Indian citizens and who have either passed or are appearing in the Bachelor’s degree final examination in any discipline from any recognized university with Mathematics and / or Statistics as one of the subjects in 10+2+3 pattern (except BBA) subject to the condition that they have to appear and qualify in the Written Test to be conducted by the “West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board.”.

Exam Pattern: The written test (consisting of two parts- Part-A: Mathematics; Part-B: Aptitude Test )

How to Apply:
Application: Candidates intending to appear in the said Joint Entrance Examination (JECA- 2009) shall have to apply in the prescribed form which will be available (along with Information Brochure) during the period 11.05.2009 to 25.05.2009 from the Branches of UNITED BANK OF INDIA at Burdwan (Station Rd. Br.), Berhampur, Bolpur, Chinsurah, Durgapur, Darjeeling, Jalpaiguri, Kalyani, Kharagpur, Malda, Purulia, Siliguri, Tamluk and at Kolkata – College Street, Jadavpur, Hatibagan, Salt Lake (Mayuk Bhavan) and BESUS (Howrah)  on all working  days including   Saturday on  payment of Rs. 400.00 (Rupees four hundred) only in CASH. Application form once purchased cannot be returned or replaced.
The forms may also be obtained from the Office of the BOARD through Postal request only by sending a Demand Draft of Rs. 400/- (four hundred) in favour of ‘West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board’ payable at Kolkata / Howrah along with a self-addressed envelope (26 cm x 13 cm) affixing postal stamp of Rs. 50/- so as to reach West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board, AQ-13/1, Sector – V, Salt Lake City, Kolkata– 700 091 on or before 18.05.2009. The Application From may also be obtained from the Board’s Office only on payment of a late fee of Rs. 50/- (Rupees fifty only) in addition to the actual cost of the form. The whole amount (i.e. Rs.450/-) is to be paid through a Bank Draft to be drawn in favour of “West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board” payable at Kolkata / Howrah from 26.05.2009 to 29.05.2009. The application form once purchased cannot be returned or replaced.


Submission : The duly filled in application form has to be sent by Speed Post only, addressed to the Member Secretary, West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board, AQ-13/1, Sector – V, Salt Lake City, Kolkata – 700 091 so as to reach the Office of the Board by 01.06.2009. There is no provision for receiving of Application Form by hand at Board’s Office.
