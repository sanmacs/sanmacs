MCA ENTRANCE NOTIFICATION

 MANIPAL UNIVERSITY

Admission for M.C.A. 2014

Testdate: 20.06.2014

Candidates are required to book their slots as per the schedule given below. Please note that the online test booking system will not allow the candidate to book a slot after the booking end date.
BOOKING START DATE : 06.06.2014
BOOKING END DATE : 11.06.2014

Last Date for Receipt of Applications: 31.05.2014

Eligibility:

  • Citizenship: Indian nationals can apply under the General Category.

  • Qualifications: The candidate must have a bachelors degree in any discipline from a recognized university. In addition, candidates must have studied Mathematics/Statistics/Computer Science as one of the subjects in 10+2 (PUC) level or Mathematics/Statistics in bachelors degree. They should have secured not less than 50% marks in aggregate.

Exam Pattern:
The test duration is of 1.30 hours and consists of 100 multiple choice questions (MCQ) of the objective type. The subjects and approximate distribution of questions is as follows: Mental Ability (25), Basic Mathematics (10), General English (25) and Computer Aptitude (40).

Test Centres:
Ahmedabad, Allahabad, Asansol, Bangalore, Belgaum, Bhillai, Bhopal, Bhubaneshwar, Chandigarh, Chennai, Coimbatore, Dehradun, Delhi, Dubai, Ernakulam, Gangtok, Ganjam, Gulbarga, Gurgaon, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Indore, Jabalpur, Jaipur, Kanpur, Kolkata, Kota, Kozhikode, Lucknow, Ludhiana, Mangalore, Manipal, Meerut, Mumbai, Nagpur, Noida, Patna, Portblair, Pune, Ranchi, Surat, Thiruvananthapuram, Udaipur, Varanasi, Vijayawada, Vizag
