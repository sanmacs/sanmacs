MCA ENTRANCE NOTIFICATION

 JAMIA MILLIA ISLAMIA

Admission for M.C.A. 2014

Testdate: 09-06-2014 Monday           Timings: 03:00 - 04:45 pm

Eligibility:
Bachelor’s degree with 50% marks in aggregate or equivalent grade and Mathematics at 10+2 level.

Total Seats: 50

Lastdate for submission of Application Forms: 28-04-2014 (Monday)

Last Date of OMR Form Submission/Acceptance of hardcopies of Online forms: 08-05-2014 (Thursday)

Application Fee : Rs. 300/- (Includes Cost of Prospectus, Application Form and Entrance Test Fee)

Application Forms:

  1. OMR Forms: Candidates seeking admission to any Program (except Ph.D.) in Jamia Millia Islamia may obtain the prescribed OMR Application Form along with the Prospectus from the University Counter at Jamia Millia Islamia (JMI) on cash payment of Application Fee as mentioned

  2. Online Forms: The Admission Application Form may also be submitted On-line on JMI’s website http://jmi.ac.in/onlineadmissionform. Candidates applying through online mode must read instructions available on the webpage for online submission.

  3. The Application Fee is non-refundable and no correspondence on this subject will be entertained.

Submission of Application Form:

  1. Candidates are required to fill up the Application Form carefully in their own writing or online (wherever applicable) giving all requisite details.

  2. Application on prescribed form completed in all respects for all programs should be submitted either in person or by post/courier, in the Office of the respective Faculty/ Department /Centre on or before the last date of submission.

  3. In case of On-line submission, the printed copy (hardcopy) of the application must reach the respective Faculty/ Department /Centre on or before the last date of submission otherwise the candidature of applicant shall be rejected.

  4. Application forms will be accepted during working hours on all working days. The Jamia remains closed on Saturday and Sunday besides other holidays recognised by it.

  5. The candidate should ensure that the application form is sent well in advance so as to reach the concerned office on or before the last date of submission. The University will not be responsible for postal delays.

  6. If a candidate intentionally or otherwise marks multiple categories for his/her candidature or does not mark any category, JMI shall consider such candidates as “General”

  7. It is the sole responsibility of the candidate to ensure his/her eligibility before applying for admission to a particular program. No claim of any sort in this matter shall be entertained.

  8. The candidate must have passed the qualifying examination from a board/university/institute recognized by JMI. Jamia Millia Islamia recognises degrees of all central universities, state universities, institutions of national importance and foreign universities, provided that their equivalence has been established by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU). Besides, the university also recognizes certain qualifications from the reputed madaris as well, the details of which are given in the prospectus.

  9. Candidates whose results for the qualifying examinations have not been declared may also apply. However, they must submit the required certificates etc. by the notified last date for this purpose as given in the prospectus or as notified on the University website.

  10. Candidates are strongly advised to keep copies of Application Form, proof of having dispatched the application form, acknowledgement receipt for facilitating issue of Duplicate Admit Cards, if required.
