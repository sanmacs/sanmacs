MCA ENTRANCE NOTIFICATION

 VELLORE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, VELLORE - MCA 2014

Admission for M.C.A. 2014

COMPUTER BASED TEST (CBT) : 7th and 8th June, 2014
Time : 10.00 am – 12.00 noon & 2.30 pm – 4.30 pm

Eligibility:

  1. Recognized Bachelor’s Degree of Minimum 3 Yrs duration in BCA, B.Sc (IT/Computer Science) with Mathematics as a course at 10+2 level or at Graduate Level. Obtained at least 60% at the qualifying Examination.

  2. Consistent Record in X and XII Std is required.

  3. Candidates appearing for their final degree exam / final semester exam in the current year are also eligible to apply.

  4. Candidates should have completed their final Semester/year exams before the selection interview at VIT University.

  5. It is compulsory for candidates who are selected to produce their final year marks’ statement and Provisional degree certificate before August 15th, 2014, failing in this attempt will lead to the cancellation of the admission.

Application Forms :
Candidates can apply through online at www.vit.ac.in/vitmee
Your application can be sent through either Registered Post or Speed Post

Mode of payment :
Select the appropriate mode of payment as DD mode or Netbanking / Debit card / Credit card Mode

Steps to be followed, if application fee is paid through Demand Draft

  1. Get ready with a computerized Demand draft for Rs. 950/- drawn from any Nationalized Bank in favour of the 'VIT University', payable at Vellore

  2. Write your Application Number, Name, Address and Mobile Number on the back side of the Demand Draft.

  3. Fill the details of Demand Draft (like DD number, DD Date, Bank Name etc,.) on the system.

  4. Send the printout of filled-out application form after affixing signature along with the DD (computerized DD).

Steps to be followed, if application fee is paid through Online payment gateway i.e., Net Banking / Credit card / Debit card Facility:

  1. Select the mode of payment as referred

  2. After submitting the payment information, candidate should wait for the intimation from the server, DO NOT press back or Refresh button in order to avoid double charge.

  3. On successful completion of transaction, acknowledgement receipt with the transaction number will be generated and you can take the printout of the acknowledgement receipt along with the application form.

Test Slot booking instructions:

  1. VITMEE 2014 test slot booking is a web based online scheduling system.

  2. Once the candidate submits the online application, the candidate will receive an e-mail (as per the id specified in the VITMEE-2014 online application form) with a website link and the corresponding login credentials (user id & password)

  3. Candidates are required to check the VIT website (www.vit.ac.in) on a regular basis for any important updates.

  4. The online test slot booking will be opened during the last week of May 2014 and the exact date will be updated in VIT Website

  5. Candidates can then login to the link, choose from the available options on test schedule (date & time), test center and book the test slot as per their choice.

  6. The schedule booking will be done on first-come- first serve basis, subject to the availability of slots / seats

  7. A slot once booked cannot be changed. Requests for change of date, slot or test center will not be entertained under any circumstance.

  8. In case of non availability of slots in a center of choice, the candidates may choose to book a slot in an alternate center.

  9. It is the responsibility of the candidate to book their test schedule in VITMEE-2014 in the given time frame. VIT will not be held accountable for the non booking of test slot by the candidate.

  10. A confirmation mail will be automatically sent to the e-mail address of the candidate, who has booked his/her schedule through the VITMEE- 2014 booking system.

The Entrance Examination will be held in the following Test Cities and Code:
BANGALORE 10, BHOPAL 11, CHENNAI 12, CHANDIGARH 13, COIMBATORE 14, HYDERABAD 15, JAIPUR 16, KOCHI 17, KOLKATA 18, LUCKNOW 19, MADURAI 20, NAGPUR 21, NEW DELHI 22, PATNA 23, PUNE 24, VELLORE 25, VIJAYAWADA 26
