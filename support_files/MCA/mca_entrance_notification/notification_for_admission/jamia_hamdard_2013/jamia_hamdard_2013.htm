MCA ENTRANCE NOTIFICATION

 JAMIA HAMDARD UNIVERSITY

Admission for M.C.A. 2013

Test Date : 26.5.2013 (Sunday)          Timings: Evening 3.30 PM to 5.30 PM

Eligibility:
A candidate must have:

  1. Passed Bachelor’s degree examination in any discipline from a recognized institution securing at least 50% marks in aggregate.

  2. Passed at least one paper of Mathematics at Bachelor level Or at Senior Secondary (10 + 2) level.

  3. Appeared in the Entrance Test conducted by Jamia Hamdard.

Total Seats: 60 (Inclusive of seats reserved for NRI/Sponsored candidates)

Selection procedure:
An Entrance Test will be conducted. The Entrance Test paper will be based on questions set on the general syllabus of BCA.

Instructions For Online Application

Step 1 : Selecting the course you want to apply

  1. An applicant shall read the Instructions & Eligibility requirements carefully before filling the application

  2. In the homepage, Select the applicant type (Indian or NRI/Foreign/Sponsored).

  3. For Post Graduate/BA (Islamic Studies)/BCA programmes you can select upto 5 Preferences

  4. An Applicant must have to read and check the application checklist before proceeding to fill the form.

Step 2 : Filling the Application Form:

  1. In the application form, Fields with * Mark are Mandatory.

  2. Fill the required fields in each section. Provide valid E-mail ID & Mobile number. All your application details will be sent to the Email Id & Mobile number mentioned in the application form.

  3. Keep your photograph, Signature & thumb impression scanned (Left hand thumb impression for Boys & Right hand thumb impression for Girls) . Upload the same in the application form. Incase you don't have your photograph, signature & thumb impression scanned, then after completing the application form, take the printout of the form & paste your images & send the form to the address mentioned below"

  4. After filling all the required fields & uploading photos, Click on Proceed Button. You will be taken to the application preview page. Verify your data, if you want to make any changes, then click on "Edit the form". If the data is fine, then click on "Proceed"

  5. Click on "Proceed" button to view the summary & Click on "Continue to payment" to proceed to make payments.

Step 3 : Making the Payment Option

  1. The Payment options available for Online applicants are

    1. Online payment (Credit card, Debit card & Netbanking)

    2. Pay by Demand Draft(DD). Student can choose any of the payment option to complete the application.

  2. When you make payment through Debit card/Credit card/Netbanking, Then, once your payment is received, Application ID will be generated instantly. Your application be processed further immediately without delay.

  3. When you make payment through Demand draft (DD), Application ID will be generated instantly. Your application be on hold, till we receive your fee payment. If Payment is not received before last day, then your application would be rejected. The DD should be drawn in favor of "Jamia Hamdard Payable at New Delhi" and send it to "the address mentioned below " along with your application print out.

  4. Keep your application ID safe. Your Application ID will be used for all further communication with the University. Quote your application ID when you call our helpline or visit the university.

Reservation/ Relaxation:
Jamia Hamdard reserves 50% seats in all courses. Other reservation and / or relaxation to various categories of applicants will be available as per the University rules. Candidates will be required to produce original certificates at the time of admission. Weightage in merit for courses is given to internal students, sports persons (National or State level) and Physically Challenged candidates as per University rules.
