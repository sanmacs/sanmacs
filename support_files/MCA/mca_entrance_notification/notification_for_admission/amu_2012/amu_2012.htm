MCA ENTRANCE NOTIFICATION

 ALIGARH MUSLIM UNIVERSITY

Admission for M.C.A. 2012

Testdate: 01-06-2012         Duration: 2 Hours        Timings:10 a.m. - 12 noon

Eligibility:
B. Tech. / B. Arch. / B.E. with 55% marks in aggregate
OR

  1. Bachelor Degree in Science from a recognised University with 55% marks in aggregate and must have studied Mathematics along with any two of the following subjects - Statistics, Physics, Chemistry, Computer Application, Computer Maintenance, Information Technology as main or subsidiary subject at the qualifying examination.

  2. Must have studied Physics and Mathematics in the Senior Secondary School Certificate or its equivalent Course.

Exam Pattern:
There shall be one objective type paper of 100 marks comprising 100 Questions with the following breakup:

a) Mathematics and Statistics

: 50 Questions

b) Physics

: 20 Questions

c) Computer Science

: 20 Questions

d) Logical ability, deductive ability and reasoning

: 10 Questions

Procedure for submission of application form :

  1. Candidates must have the Cash receipt/Demand Draft of requisite amount in favour of Finance Officer, AMU, Payable at Aligarh with him as candidates are barred to proceed further without filling up the Test Fee/Processing Charges Column. The Drafts should preferably be from the Nationalized Banks.

  2. The candidate, after his/her online Form submission, will be allotted a Transaction ID & password that will be shown to him/her on the screen and the same will be mailed to the email ID he/she has mentioned in the Application Form. Howsoever, he/she may note it down for his/her convenience. He/she may also open/save the PDF file by clicking the link shown on the screen below the username & password.

  3. The candidate is required to send the duly filled in Application Form/printout of the generated PDF File to Deputy Controller (Admission Section), Controllers Office, AMU, Aligarh  202002 on or before the last date. Before sending the Printout of the Form to the Admission Section, the candidate must ensure that he/she has:

    • Affixed his/her recent photograph on the Form on the required places.

    • Put his/her thumb impression and Signature in the boxes provided for the purpose.

    • Appended the Signature after going through the declaration.

    • Attached the DD/C.R. and all the required documents (High school certificate Marksheets etc.) with the Application Form.

    • Written his/her Name, Father's name & Course name & Transaction ID at the back of the Draft.

    • Attached No Objection Certificate from the employer, if employed.

    • Attached attested Certificate in support of special categories.

  4. All photographs must be identical, snapped with front profile showing full head, face, shoulder and with both ears and taken without wearing any Cap/Hat/Sunglasses. Photograph must have been taken not earlier than 3 months.
