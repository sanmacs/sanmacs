MCA ENTRANCE NOTIFICATION

 HARYANA COMBINED ONLINE ENTRANCE TEST

Admissions for MCA - 2015

Dates of OLET Entrance Test: 21.05.2015 to 23.05.2015

Eligibility:
A. Minimum Academic Qualifications for MCA

Pass in any recognized Bachelor’s Degree of minimum 3 Yrs duration with Mathematics at 10+2 level.

OR

Pass in any recognized Bachelor’s Degree of minimum 3 Yrs duration with Mathematics as one of the subjects.

OR

Should be pass in BCA Degree of minimum three years duration from a recognized University Obtained at least 50% (47.5% in case of candidate belonging to reserved category) in the qualifying Examination.

Note: Candidates having passed Mathematics / Statistics in one semester / in one year as the case may be shall be considered eligible for MCA.
For Kashmiri Migrants (KM) relaxation in cut-off percentage upto 10% subject to minimum eligibility requirement.

Basis of admission: Admissions shall be made on the basis of the inter se merit of Online Entrance Test (MCA- 2015) conducted by HSTES

Scheme and Syllabi:

(A) Scheme of Entrance Test:

(i) Maximum marks : 90

(ii) There will be one Question Paper of 90 minutes duration in three parts as under:

(iii) Only objective type / multiple-choice questions with one correct answer will be asked in the Entrance Test

(iv) There will “NOT" be any Negative marking.

(B) Syllabus: 10+2 Level Mathematics and English / Comprehension. Besides, questions will be asked to test Logical & Analytical Ability.

Important Information:

  1. Admissions to MCA regular course shall be made on the basis of the inter se merit of Online Entrance Test to be conducted for MCA-15by HSTES for the session 2015-16

  2. The Online entrance test fee or Application fee (in case of KM) for MCA-2015 shall be deposited by the candidate through e-challan generated from www.onlinetesthry.gov.in during filling of the online application form. The candidate shall take print out of e-Challan and shall deposit on the next working day of generation of the e-challan in any branch of following designated banks:

    I. Punjab National Bank
    II. Axis Bank
    The Online entrance test fee or Application: Rs. 500/- for General Category
    Rs. 200/- For all Reserved Categories SC/BC/SBC/EBP/PH/FF/ESM/GIRLS/KM)
    The Online Entrance Test Fee or Application fee once paid shall not be refunded (full or partial) under any circumstances.

  3. An e-Challan of selected banks for depositing the entrance test fee or application fee will be generated by the candidate from the website: www.onlinetesthry.nic.in . The candidate will take the printout of e-challan and will deposit the applicable fee (OLET fee or Application fee) on next day of e-Challan generation in the designated bank and should receive the receipt of duly stamped by the bank

  4. Before submitting the online application form, the candidate can check and edit his/her registration details. Further, the candidate should also ensure that the photograph & signature uploaded are his/her own photograph & signature and registration details of the candidate must be same as registered in qualifying exam. The incorrect information submitted in online application form shall not be entertained and in such a case the candidate is required to resubmit a fresh online application form and the fee should also be deposited against the fresh application form

  5. The counseling fee for MCA course is Rs. 500/- (Five Hundred Rupees) only (Non- Refundable) for all category candidates
