MCA ENTRANCE NOTIFICATION

 GURUKULA KANGRI VISHWAVIDYALAYA

Admission for M.C.A. 2015

Testdate: 29-06-2015            Timings: 8:30 a.m. - 10:30 a.m.

Eligibility:
Graduate with minimum 50% marks and Mathematics as one subject at the Intermediate (10+2) level.

Examination Centre

01 Bhopal, 02 Chandigarh, 03 Dehradun, 04 Delhi, 05 Haridwar, 06 Lucknow

Note: Examination centre may be changed if the number of the candidate are less than 50

How to Apply:

Application form and Information Brochure for entrance examination can be obtained from the office of Registrar, Gurukula Kangri Vishwavidyalaya, Haridwar by paying Rs. 600/- in cash or by sending a demand draft of Rs. 650/- to the Registrar, Gurukula Kangri Vishwavidyalaya, Haridwar. The demand draft should be in favour of "Registrar,Gurukula Kangri Vishwavidyalaya" payable at Haridwar.

The candidates appearing in the final year of qualifying examination (except N.R.I./Sponsored/N.R.I. Sponsored /Foreign candidates) are also eligible to apply. Such candidates will have to submit compulsorily their complete final result of qualifying examination upto 15 October 2015, failing which their admission shall be treated as cancelled.

Filled in application form for entrance examination may be submitted to the office of Registrar, Gurukula Kangri Vishwavidyalaya, Haridwar. Candidate may also download application form from Vishwavidyalaya website & submit completely filled in application alongwith demad draft of Rs. 600/-

Note:

  1. No examination fee shall be paid by the candidate while submitting application form. However, downloaded application from website will only be accepted alongwith requisite fee (Rs. 600/-).

  2. Write the name of the course applied on the top of the envelop and your name and course on the back of the demand draft while demanding application form

  3. Sponsored/N.R.I./N.R.I. Sponsored /Foreign candidates will have to submit compulsorily the complete result of the qualifying examination along with application form.
