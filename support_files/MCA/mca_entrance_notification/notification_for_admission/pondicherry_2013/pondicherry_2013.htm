MCA ENTRANCE NOTIFICATION

 PONDICHERRY UNIVERSITY

Admission for M.C.A. 2013

Testdate: 26.05.2013        Timings: 2.00 p.m. to 4.00 p.m.

Eligibility:
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Applications/ Computer Science/ Information Technology or Bachelor’s degree in Commerce/ Corporate Secretaryship/ Economics / Business Administration with Mathematics/ Business Mathematics / Statistics /Computer Applications as one of the subjects or Bachelor’s degree in Science with Mathematics/ Statistics as one of the subjects with a minimum of 55% marks

Intake:
120 (Puducherry)
40 (Karikal)

How to Apply:

  1. ONLINE: Application form can be submitted online through the University website www.pondiuni.edu.in by following the instructions given therein. Application fee must be credited at Pondicherry University Account in any of the following bank all over India under Core Banking System on payment of the cost of Application Form plus Bank charges as per the instructions given in the website.
    1. Any of the branches of INDIAN BANK with instruction to credit it to MUP Account No. 6107804872
    OR
    2. Any of the branches of STATE BANK OF INDIA with instruction to credit it to POWER JYOTI Account No. 32225505012

  2. BY POST: For Application Form by post write with course name to The Deputy Registrar (Academic II), Dr. B.R.Ambedkar Administrative Building, Pondicherry University, R.V. Nagar, Kalapet, Puducherry 605 014. Along with a self addressed envelop of size 32cm x 25cm, stamped for Rs.90/- and DD for the prescribed application fee drawn in favour of the Finance Officer, Pondicherry University, Payable at Puducherry. Request for application must be superscribed on the envelope “Request for application form for admission..” (Course name)

  3. IN PERSON The application forms can also be obtained from the Pondicherry University Staff Co-operative Credit Society at the University campus on payment of the cost of the application form plus Rs. 10/- as service charge.

Application Fee:
For all courses Rs.100/-, Rs. 50/- for SC/ST, Exempted for differently abled candidates - subject to submission of relevant certificates.

Submission:
Return the filled in application forms by Registered/ Speed Post or in Person to the Deputy Registrar (Academic-II), Dr. B. R. Ambedkar Administrative Building, Pondicherry University, R.Venkataraman Nagar, Kalapet, Puducherry - 605014 so as to reach him on or before 18th April 2013. Superscribe the envelope as “Application for admission to ....................... (Name of the course)” Candidates applying for more than one course should send their applications in separate covers.
