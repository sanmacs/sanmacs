MCA ENTRANCE NOTIFICATION

 Andhra Pradesh Integrated Common Entrance Test (APICET) - 2015

Admission for M.C.A. 2015

Testdate: 16-05-2015 (Saturday)         Timings: 10.00AM to 12.30 P.M.

Applications are invited for appearing at the Andhra Pradesh Integrated Common Entrance Test (APICET-2015) for admission into M.B.A. and M.C.A. courses (full time/parttime/ evening/distance mode) offered by the following Universities in the State of Andhra Pradesh including their affiliated colleges for the academic year 2015-2016 to be conducted by Andhra University, Visakhapatnam (as per section 11 (2) (II) (vi) of the Act No: 16 of 1981 of the Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education and in pursuance of the powers conferred under rule 2 (I) (vii) of the rules issued in the G.0. Ms.No.25 Higher Education (EC.1) Dept Dt: 19.03.04 and amendments for the Academic Year 2015.2016).

1. Andhra University (AU), 2. Sri Venkateswara University (SVU), 3. Sri Padmavati Mahila Vishwavidyalayam(SPMV), 4. Sri Krishnadevaraya University (SKDU), 5. Dr. B.R.Ambedkar University (BRAU), 6. JNT University, Kakinada (JNTU-K), 7. JNT University, Anantapur (JNTU-A), 8. Adikavi Nannaya University(ANUR), 9. Acharya Nagarjuna University (ANU), 10. Krishna University (KU), 11. Vikram Simhapuri University (VSU), 12. Rayalaseema University (RSU), 13. Yogi Vemana University (YVU)

Eligibility:

  1. The candidates should be Indian Nationals and should satisfy local / non-local status requirements laid down in the Andhra Pradesh State Educational Institutions (Regulations of Admission) Order, 1974 as amended up to date (as per G.O. Ms. No. 25, dated: 19.03.2004 & G.O.Ms.No.108, dated 3-7-2005,Higher Education Department, dated 30-7-2005 and its amendments).

  2. The candidates other than Indian Nationals should satisfy the rules of the Universities concerned

  3. The candidates seeking admission -

    1. For MCA: Should have passed recognized Bachelors Degree examination of minimum three years duration with at least 50% marks (45% marks in case of reserved categories) in the qualifying examination with Mathematics at 10+2 level.

  4. The qualifying Degree obtained by Distance Mode Program should have recognition by joint committee of UGC, AICTE and DEC.

  5. The candidates who are appearing for the final year degree examination shall also be eligible to appear for APICET-2015

  6. Admission into any college depends on its Recognition/Affiliation by the Statutory Bodies.

  7. Qualifying marks in APICET-2015 is 25% and no minimum marks are prescribed for SC/ST candidates.

Mere qualification in the entrance test does not entitle a candidate to find admission into MBA/MCA course unless the candidate:

  1. appears for Counseling at the notified Centers

  2. applies in response to the admission notification issued by the authority concerned (for part-time\evening\distance mode)

  3. satisfies all the eligibility criteria for admission as stipulated by the authority concerned

Submission of Online Applications :

APICET-2015 Applications should be submitted through ONLINE mode only. The Registration fee for online submission and late fee, if applicable, may be paid at AP Online Centers in Andhra Pradesh. and also through paymentgateway (Credit Card / Debit Card).
