UNIVERSITY OF JAMMU

HOSTEL FACILITY

General Information and Regulations relating to Residence of Students in Hostel

A) General Information :-

1.

The University maintains hostels for men and women students. The number of boarders that the hostel can accommodate is as under :-

i)

Boys Hostel, University Old Campus

97

ii)

Nehru Hall, University New Campus

122

iii)

New Boys Hostel University New Campus

120

iv)

Girls Hostel (Old) University New Campus

150/160

v)

Girls Hostel New

90

2.

Each room in the hostels is fitted with a ceiling electric fan and each boarder is provided a cot, a table and a chair for use in the hostels.

3.

The TV/Common room remains open during such hours as are notified from time to time by the Warden of the Hostel.

4.

In order to keep the expenses reasonably low, the hostel messes are run by the boarders on a co-operative basis or on a contract basis. The University provides Cooks/Ward Boys/ Maid Servants and utensils from its grant. The running of mess will be the responsibility of the mess committee appointed by the Warden.

5.

The schedule of hostel dues and securities payable by each boarder at the time of admission is as under :-

Double Seated Rooms

Rent for one seat per month

25

Electricity charges per month

20

Miscellaneous charges per month

20

Maintenance fee per month

30

Single Seated Rooms

Rent

Rs. 30/- per month

Electricity charges

Rs. 20/- per month

Miscellaneous charges

Rs. 20/- per month

Maintenance fee

Rs. 30/- per month

Other Charges

Medical Fee

Rs. 20/- p.a.

Social function fee

Rs. 100/- p.a.

Annual function fee (the excess expenditure on annual function shall have to be worne by the Boarder)

Rs. 80/- p.a.

Hostel Security

Rs. 200/- p.a.

Mess Security

Rs. 500/- p.a.

General Breakage charges

Rs. 50/- p.m.

Picnic fee

Rs. 50/- p.a.

There are two Health Centres one in the University Old Campus and other in the University New Campus.

Note :
The hostels remain closed during summer vacations. The boarders shall have to vacate the rooms during summer vacations for annual repairs of the rooms failing which a rent @ Rs. 50/- p.m. will be charged for this period.
