IMPORTANT INSTRUCTIONS :

i) Please fill in the application form carefully and send it by registered post / by hand to the Head, Department of Computer Science, New Campus, University of Jammu, Jammu - 180 006, so as to reach him not later than the date shown in the advertisement.

ii) Incomplete / late application will not be entertained.

iii) Eligible candidates shall be issued Admit Cards by post or by hand on request.

iv) Eligible candidates will be allowed to appear in the Entrance Test on the production of admit card. Those who do not receive their admit card four days prior to the commencement of written test should contact the Department of Computer Science in this regard and collect the duplicate admit card personally after submitting attested passport size photograph.

v) Please attach a certificate of good moral character from the Head of the institution last attended or from a Gazetted Officer. Also attach attested true copy of marks certificate of qualifying examination.

vi) Any one found using unfair means in the Entrance Test shall be disqualified and shall loose eligibility for future also.

vii) The candidates applying to seek admission under any reserved category must attach with the application form the attested copies of appropriate certificates issued by the competent authority.

viii) The candidates selected for admission shall have to produce certificate in original, failing which their admission shall be cancelled.

ix) Admission / Selection to the MCA course shall be provisional, subject to the verification of original certificates and related documents.

x) If a selected candidate fails to produce original certificates or deposit the required fee within the specified period of time, his selection shall be cancelled and the seat falling vacant shall be allotted to the next candidate in the merit.

xi) The candidates should remain in touch with the PG Dept. of Computer Science, University of Jammu regarding the declaration of the Common Entrance Test result, merit list and related other informations.

xii) For determining the number of seats in different reserved categories, fraction of less than half shall be ignored and fraction of a half and more shall be treated as one, subject to the condition that a minimum of one seat is reserved in each of the reserved categories as listed under reserved categories.

xiii) The candidates having any grievance, may apply on the appellate form available from the University on payment of fee. The appellate form with all the formalities completed is to be submitted to Dean Academic Affairs within the dates notified by the University.

xiv) Enclose the list of documents attached with the application form.

Note :
In case of any discrepancy / dispute, not withstanding anything contained in the admission brochure, the statutes / regulations governing admission to MCA course in conjunction with general statutes / regulations governing admissions to other Master's Degree programmes of the University shall be followed.
