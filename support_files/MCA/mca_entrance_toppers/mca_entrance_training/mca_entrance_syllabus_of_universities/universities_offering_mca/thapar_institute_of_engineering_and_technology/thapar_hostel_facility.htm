THAPAR INSTITUTE OF ENGG. & TECH.

HOSTEL FACILITY

The institute has four boys and two girl hostels. To develop the overall personality of students each hostel works on motto of "home away home". Out of these four there are three undergraduate and one post graduate hostel. Every hostel has its own indoor- outdoor games facility, libraries, reading rooms, common room, T.V. room, cyber café and gymnasium. There is a cooperative messing system in all the hostels. Students are actively involved in all hostel activities through the different committees.
