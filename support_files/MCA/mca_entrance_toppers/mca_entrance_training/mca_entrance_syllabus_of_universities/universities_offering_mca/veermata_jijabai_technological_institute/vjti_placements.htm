PLACEMENT & TRAINING

The products of the institute have always been well received by the industries. Students of final year diploma/degree/postgraduate courses are selected and offered posts even before they appear for their final year examinations. The close rapport developed by the Training & Placement department with industries through such activities as in-plant training of degree as well as diploma students, as well as regular visits to various organizations by the Training & Placement officer has helped the students' community in securing gainful employment. Every year, more than 100 campus interviews are conducted for various courses.

Most of the industries are really happy with our students' performance in terms of personality, communication, general awareness, technical capabilities etc. Almost all eligible students receive their job offer one year in advance. The salary offered by various organizations ranges from Rs.1,20,000/- to Rs.4,80,000 per annum. Various manufacturing, construction as well as IT companies visit our campus.
