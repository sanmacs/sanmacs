The distribution of the seats at different Dept./inst. is as follows :

  • PG. Dept. of Computer Science, University of Jammu :

    • The 67% of the total seats belong to open merit category and the remaining 33% of the seats are reserved for the candidates belonging to the following researved categories, which shall be filled as per the relevant statutes governing the general admissions of the University.

      • Candidates belonging to Scheduled caste.

      • Spertsman and person who have excelled in Cultural/ Literary Activities.

      • Person who have participated with distinction in N.C.C. & N.S.S. Activities.

      • Defence Service Personal including ex-servicemen and their children who are permanent residents of the state and have had a satisfactory service record.

      • Candidates belonging to the areas of the Actual Line of Control.

      • Candidates belonging to Scheduled Tribe.

      • Candidate belonging to Backward Areas other than Leh & Kargil.

      • Candidates belonging to district of Leh & Kargil.

      • Candidates belonging to Weak & Underprivileged classes as notified in S.R.O. by the State Govt. from time to time.

    • The Open category seats are to be filled by the candidates of Jammu University as well as by the candidates from other than Jammu University with the condition that not more than half the seats shall be allocated to the candidates from other than Jammu University provided they fall in the open merit list.

    • The Vice-chancellor may at its discretion, authorize admission of one orthopaedically handicapped candidate, who is suffering a degree of disability of 45% and above, subject to the fulfillment of the eligibility criteria.

    • The University may, subject to the fulfillment of the eligibility conditions, consider the admission of not more than five candidates belonging to Kashmiri Migrant families, in order of merit, not exceeding one in a particular course, provided that such a candidate produces a documentary evidence of belonging to family of registered as Kashmiri Migrant. Admission under this category may be allowed only in the course for which classes are not being conducted by the post graduate programme of Kashmir University at Jammu University Campus. Such admission may be authorized by the Dean Academic Affairs against a seat, which shall be supernumerary in a course of study in the department.

    • The University offers a limited number of Payment Seats in the MCA Programme in the PG Dept. of Computer Science which shall be filled as per the relevant statutes / regulations governing admissions. These, however, shall be deemed as supernumerary seats i.e. over and above the normal number of seats mentioned earlier and thus, shall not be reckoned in determining the quantum of reserved and open merit seats. A candidate seeking admission under the payment seat shall have to declare his / her intention at the appropriate column in the Application Form and if selected shall have to deposit Rs. 2 Lacs at the time of admission in the Department besides the normal fee / charges for the course. The first preference shall be given to the candidates from Jammu University or J&K State Subject holders.

  • Bhadarwah Campus, University of Jammu and Govt. Degree College, Rajouri :
    In each of the above two places the 67% of the total seats belong to Open Merit Category and the remaining 33% of the seats are reserved for the cadidates belonging to different reserved categories as per the norms as applicable for admission in PG Department of Computer Science, University of Jammu.

  • Mahant Bachittar Singh College of Engineering & Technology (MBS) and Model Institute of Engineering & technology (MIET), Jammu :
    There are 25 seats in each college which shall be filled in the Open Category only, in accordance with merit as per the relevant statutes governing the general admissions of the University.
