CUET (PG) 2024 - All India Mock Testing

CUET (PG) 2024 is around the corner.

The importance of scoring top marks in CUET(PG) is imperative as it opens doors of top courses in top universities for you.

Computer Based Tests ( CBTs) conducted in 325 test cities globally (India and abroad) for admission to around 160 test papers and courses in more than 180 Universities, enable lakhs of students to pursue their dream of higher studies from the top Universities in the subject of their choice.

However CBT has some challenges too. The pattern, style and functionality is different from the one used in pen-paper format. An adequate practice is necessary in The CBT format that too a replica of NTA test portal for attempting the actual CBT effectively and efficiently.

SANMACS, an EdTech, is offering an opportunity to students to appear in All India MOCKS for all courses on a platform exactly same as that of NTA. The MOCKS are full-length tests (FLTs) prepared using actual exam questions and give sufficient practice of the CBT mode to increase your chances manifolds.
