GROW WITH US

SANMACS India, a dedicated academia and a pioneer in MCA Entrance Guidance with superb results over more than a decade and having extensive work experience with international affiliates have now decided to expand its operations throughout India.

SANMACS INDIA is interested in working with Independent companies that have met the highest standards and criteria necessary to offer and deliver SANMACS EXPERTISE In test preparation program within their local markets.

The association with SANMACS will help its partners to capitalize on the SANMACS name and fame and being able to run successful educational ventures for mutual interests and benefits.

PREREQUISITES FOR SANMACS PARTNERS

  • Commitment to helping individuals achieve their educational and career goals.

  • Sufficient financial resources to launch, support and grow the program.

  • Adequate facilities with up-to-date technical and delivery resources.

  • Market expertise in education and career service programs.

  • Good, ethical and reputable standing in the local market.

HOW IT WORKS

  • Interested companies / individuals will need to submit a preliminary application to SANMACS INDIA for evaluation and, if approved, will then need to complete and submit a formal business plan for final approval.

  • SANMACS INDIA shall provide its associates with the necessary training, tools and academic resourses to ensure their success in their respective territory.

  • The program partners shall actively market, promote, sell and deliver the SANMACS TEST preparation programs.

  • The SANMACS associates will be expected to adhere to the highest quality standards in their promotion and delivery of the SANMACS TEST preparation programs.
